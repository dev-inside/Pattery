Title: Install

====

Content:

Just extrat Pattery to your Server. Visit Pattery *http://localhost/yourfolder/*  and check out, if the "Download"-button
works. If not, just make the *"files"-directory* writeable *(chmod -777)*.

- You can get the Pack on Github:  
<https://github.com/dev-inside/pattery>

- Or on Bitbucket  
<https://bitbucket.org/devinside/pattery/>


====
